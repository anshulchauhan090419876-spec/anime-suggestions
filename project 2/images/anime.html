Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young, socially isolated ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

One Punch Man

One-Punch Man is a popular Japanese action-comedy series that follows the story of Saitama, a superhero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch. Due to his overwhelming strength, he suffers from boredom and an existential crisis, constantly searching for a worthy opponent

Black Clover

Black Clover is a Japanese manga and anime series about a world where magic is everything, and two orphans, Asta and Yuno, are in a friendly rivalry to become the next Wizard King, despite Asta having no magic
